SOLUTION & SYSTEM

RTSL - Real Time Locations Services

Our Real Time Location Tracking System (RTLS) solution includes position sensors that connect to a variety of entities, be it a patient, a staff member, or a piece of equipment. Thanks to RTLS, you can instantly track the position of the fixture or personnel in an organization.

Laundry Management Systems

The Laundry Management System allows you to track and record the work coming to your laundry by RFID, barcoding or counting unlabeled products. RFID Warehouse Laundry System will make it easier for your business to manage the inventory in the best way, to automatically monitor the laundry activity according to the customer and to sort by the customer, and to organize the uniforms of the customer employees. Using, aging and maintaining quality standards of uniforms and linens will be very easy thanks to this system.

Asset Management Systems

An RFID asset tracking system uses electromagnetic fields to transmit data from an RFID tag to a reader.
Whether you're talking about office equipment in a high-rise building, equipment in a large hospital, tools in a factory, or pumping equipment at remote oil fields, RFID can provide accurate, real-time tracking data.

Inventory Management Systems

Inventory management system is a software system that used by enterprises for inventory tracking and strategic decision. With the development of the technology of computer and network, automatic inventory management has become a necessity in modern business management. Wireless inventory management system helps to optimize each step of the warehouse work flow with much higher efficiency by using barcode and RFID technology for identifying and tracking inventory items, meanwhile saves expenses on hardware by using smartphones and integrated handheld devices.We also offer a variety of solutions for the identification of the stock items such as barcode or QR code printing, or using RFID technology, to cope with your business needs.

Warehouse Management Systems

It enables fast and controlled shipment operations such as identification in warehouses, tracking of the production line, searching-finding, counting operations of stored products with hand terminal, preventing unauthorized goods output and shipping.

Custom IoT Solutions

Telemetry Management Systems

Telemetry systems transmit measurement data reliably under various conditions. We provide turnkey solutions that are ready to use. Depending on the specifications we draw on tried and tested, standardized technology, modify if required to suit the customer’s specifications or even manufacture entirely new developments.

Product Management Systems

Production management deals with decision-making related to production processes so that the resulting goods or service is produced according to specification, in the amount and by the schedule demanded and at minimum cost. Production management also deals with decisionmaking regarding the quality, quantity, cost, etc., of production. It applies management principles to production.
