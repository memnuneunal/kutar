APPLICATION & WORK

Textile Applications - Hospital, Hotel, Retail

Laundry management solutions and laundry services: industry, health care, apparel RFID laundry management, etc. Washable labeled product security and control systems with RFID technology will help you control textile products and strengthen your company with the efficiency it will add to you. RFID Tags are Water Temperature and Pressure Resistant Long-Lasting Products You can maximize efficiency by minimizing costs by controlling many processes with a chip you will attach to a textile product.

Automotive - Inventory, Production Management

RFID in automotive industry involves attaching RFID tags to virtually any component of an operation and tracking the tagged assets using RFID software and mobile computers. Auto plant managers can attach RFID tags to tools, containers, parts, shipping boxes and other supplies allowing even the tiniest of components to be monitored and tracked at all times.

Logistic - Warehouse, Transportation

You can view and control all processes in the supply chain until distributing the information and finally distributing it to the end customer. Provides in-warehouse product control. It covers everything from door automation, condition monitoring, security and safety to real-time location systems. The benefits of RFID deployment are quickly realized and include improved operational efficiency, improved utilization, and significant ROI increases.

Health - Patient, Laundry, Inventory, Performance monitoring

Facility Management The health facility is like a small city. Regular maintenance uses RFID to tag important services. When read, the RFID tag tells the service technician what and how to perform the service. An RFID-guided maintenance program ensures that all equipment is maintained on time and technically.

Telemetry - Temperature, Humidity, Light, Noise, Air Quality, level, etc. enviroment management

Telemetry is the automatic recording and transmission of data from remote or inaccessible sources to an IT system in a different location for monitoring and analysis. Telemetry data can be transmitted using radio, infrared, ultrasonic, GSM, satellite or cable, depending on the application. If we can receive any data and process and interpret this data, even the most complex systems are simplified and controlled by telemetry systems.
